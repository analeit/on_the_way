On The Way is about taking the next step to be fully present. By checking in with yourself through a bullet journal-like experience, you can track your daily mood and thoughts. And don't worry if you're not in your best! This app adapts itself to your daily life wherever you go on your journey helping you to take a step back to see the big picture. Feel free to make it yours and keep going by your own way!

MISSION

To be a catalyst of change to dismantle taboos set by society on mental health related matters.

GOALS

Encourage a personal reflection through a daily journal entry, helping users navigate on their emotional health journey.
